OUR FEATURES

Real-time Speed Bumps Detection

Processes image feeds, identifying speed breakers. This provides immediate warnings to drivers through maps

Accurate Localization

Pinpoint the precise location of speed breakers on the road, ensuring drivers know exactly where the hazard is.

Visual Alerts

Clear and distinct visual cues are displayed on the driver's screen, highlighting the presence of a speed breaker



Integration with Navigation Systems

Seamlessly integrate our speed breaker detection data with existing navigation systems for enhanced road awarenes

Data Collection for Infrastructure Improvement

Our system gathers valuable data on speed breaker locations and conditions, providing insights for road authorities
