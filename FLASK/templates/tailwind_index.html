How BumpSafe Vision Works

A detailed breakdown of processes and mechanisms behind our speed bump detection system,
simplifying complex concepts for easy understanding.

1

Image Capture

A high-resolution camera mounted on the vehicle captures real-time images of the road ahead.

2

AI Analysis

Our advanced AI model analyzes the images, identifying potential speed bumps with high accuracy.

3

Alert Generation

The system generates timely alerts, notifying the driver of upcoming speed bumps.

4

Mapping & Location Tagging

The detected bump or hump is mapped using GPS coordinates and marked on Google Maps

5

User Feedback & Continuous Learning

Users can validate or report incorrect detections, improving AI accuracy over time.

6

Data Storage & Report Generation

The system stores detected road conditions for further analysis.
